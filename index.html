2. The National Parks

Colorado is home to many National Parks and National Forests which makes colorado a unique place to be. All of this protected land allows Colorado to have a lot of untouched natural beauty to view. Here are some of these breathtaking protected lands:

  • Rocky Mountain National Park
  • Arapaho National Forest
  • Roosevelt National Forest
  • Holy Cross Wilderness
  • Great Sand Dunes National Park

Spending time at altitude has been shown to have positive effects on the body and mind. Some benefits are:

  • Improved heart function
  • Enhanced muscle performance
  • Lowered risk of death from cancer
  • Increased dopamine
  • Faster metabolism
